## METHODOLOGY NOTES

- **Search Engines Used:** Startpage (worked initially, then rate-limited), Bing (partial), Google (JS-rendered, blocked direct scraping)
- **Direct Crawling:** Used curl to probe known institutional URLs
- **PDF Verification:** HTTP HEAD requests to confirm 200 status
- **Limitations:** Many government sites use JS rendering (React/Angular) making curl-based scraping incomplete. Browser-based verification needed for ~25 sites.
- **Language:** English only. Non-English reparations documents (Haiti, French Caribbean, Latin America) not captured.
- **Date Range:** 1865 (Field Order 15) to June 2026
